Pizza

Description

The Paneer & Onion Pizza is a very good go to pizza that everyone likes.

Put the below ingredients all together and there you go! You have a spicy cheese filled mouth watering pizza all by yourself!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Base
  • Paneer
  • Onions
  • Pizza Sause
  • Cheeza Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Take the pizza base and apply pizza sause over it.
  2. Cut panner into small cubes and onions in traditional way.
  3. Add the paneer cubes and onions on top of your pizza.
  4. Using a shredder, shred the cheeza cheese on top of your pizza
  5. Put it in the oven and select pizza option in the settings of your oven.
  6. Bookmark this page until your delicious pizza is getting ready!
  7. Once done, take your pizza out of the oven, and sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes over it.
  8. Your Paneer & Onion Pizza is ready to eat!
