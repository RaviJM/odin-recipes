Burger

Description

The Veg Burger is the most common favourite of peeps!

Put the below ingredients all together and there you go! You have a mouth watering Burger for you to eat!

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • Potato
  • Cabbage
  • Vispo's Burger Masala
  • Amul's Cheese slices

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and put them in cooker for 5-7 whistles!
  2. Cut the burger bun in half.
  3. Mash the potatoes properly and put all required masalas in it.
  4. Make small patties of the mashed potato paste and deep fry them.
  5. Apply Vispo's burger masala on the base of the burger bun.
  6. Once brown and ready, take the potatoes out & place them inside the burger bun
  7. Put a slice of cabbage over it
  8. Don't forget to put a cheese slice over the cabbage.
  9. Put it in the oven and select the Burger option in the settings.
  10. By the time your sizzling burger is cooking, don't forget to bookmark our page!
  11. Take the burger out and serve it with french fries!
  12. Your sizzling hot veg burger is ready to eat!
